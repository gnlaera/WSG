Quick Start
  1. Generate or choose a primitive lifeless planet.
  2. Inspect Life Viability, water stress, temperature, and nutrients.
  3. Use terraforming tools to create viable zones.
  4. Add minerals if fertility is limiting.
  5. Seed primitive life on a high-viability tile.
  6. Advance time and stabilise if stress rises.
